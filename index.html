Problem

Management of preschool

About project

If you have kids, you have a lot of work to do. It is an application concept that will improve your communication and management of kindergarten / nursery affairs. The goal is that all things are in one place and that they can be conveniently settled with a click, not to forget anything and save time. You can easily communicate with the instutution staff / other parents, check information about your child, pay bills and view photos.

Why this project was created?

The functioning of a child in kindergarten is an important matter for both the child and the parent, it is obvious. Information and good communication are at this point essential for good cooperation between both parties and, consequently, for the well-being of the child. However, the situation in kindergarten can be dynamic, there are not always appropriate circumstances for transmitting information, which, given the number of children, is large and it is not easy to organize and remember. Nowadays, when we use applications to make purchases, order food and communicate for business and private purposes, it seems obvious that there should be a tool that will collect and organize information for people, and enable their efficient transfer. Thanks to this, parents and guardians can save time, be more up to date with the preschooler's life, establish closer relationships with others, improve the comfort of their child thanks to better cooperation with the staff and greater commitment. Children usually spend as much time in kindergarten as their parents do at work, i.e. most of the day, in an important period of very intensive development and learning, in which the main contribution is made by the institution they attend. It is definitely worth supporting them on this path.

Problems

Modern people have too much on their minds. It's hard for them to remember everything and keep up to date. There are many children in institutions - babysitters are unable to remember all information about each of them and successfully pass them on to parents.

Hypothesis

Nowadays parents / guardians are more often involved in the life of the institution and they would like to be able to communicate with their institution on an ongoing basis. They need to make sure their children develop healthy and properly. At the same time, they don't have much time and would like to do it effortlessly.

Desk research and competition research

First of all, I decided to familiarize myself with the existing applications of this type. I chose three applications with a fairly large number of downloads - 4parents with 50,000, LiveKid with 100,000 and Inso with 10,000. All of them are used to contact the kindergarten, report absences, make payments, etc. I checked what users value most, what causes their frustration, what improvements they would like - opinions were a very valuable source of knowledge on these topics. Then I compared the applications from the visual side, assessed them according to the heuristics and highlighted their main functions. It gave me an overview of what the users' needs and pain points are, what existing and similar tools contain, what features can be added and what can be improved.

User research

Hypothesis

Nowadays, young parents are busy, work, take care of a child, a lot is required of them in these two roles, caring for a small child involves a lot of things to do and remember, every saving of time and energy on daily activities is worth its weight in gold

What we want to know?

  • How does the in the institution look like from the point of view of the parent / guardian?
  • What information does a parent / guardian need?
  • What is he satisfied with in the institution?
  • What does he have problems with on a daily basis, what complicates his life?
  • What would make it easier for him?
  • What is his experience in using the application, is he used to it, is it convenient for him or does he prefer traditional forms of dealing with matters?

Methods

In-depth interviews
Surveys

In-depth interviews

  • Do children attend the facility on a daily basis? Who is picking them up and bringing them back?
  • How often does your child's unplanned absence from the institution happen and for what reasons? What are you doing then?
  • How do you pay, is it convenient? Is it clear to you how much and for what you are paying?
  • Would you like a different form of communication with staff / parents? What? Why?
  • What information about the child do you get from the facility? What information are you missing?
  • What do you consider an advantage of your facility and what would you like to improve in it?
  • Do you use mobile applications often? (mail, photos, ordering food, shopping, instant messaging, work / hobby / entertainment tools). Is it simple or straightforward for you?
